Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-17

This Privacy Policy describes how Cutting Edge Analytics (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use Content Publishing Automation, a workflow that generates and publishes social-media content via services such as LinkedIn, Reddit, Telegram, and others (the “Service”).

If you have questions, contact us at kolby@ce-analytics.com.

Information We Collect

  • Account identifiers: IDs, usernames or public profile links needed to publish content through third-party APIs (e.g., LinkedIn organization/user ID).
  • Content you provide: source media, transcriptions, prompts, draft/final post text, and feedback for revisions.
  • Operational data: timestamps, execution logs, and minimal technical metadata required to run automations (e.g., webhook or chat IDs).
  • Credentials: API/OAuth tokens you connect. These are stored only in your automation platform’s encrypted credential store (e.g., n8n) and are not sold or shared.

How We Use Information

  • Create, edit, and publish posts to the platforms you select.
  • Authenticate with third-party APIs on your behalf.
  • Improve reliability, troubleshoot issues, and prevent abuse.
  • Comply with platform terms and applicable law.

Legal Bases

We process information with your consent and/or as necessary to perform the Service you request. Where required, we rely on legitimate interests in operating and securing the Service.

Sharing of Information

We share information only as necessary to operate the Service:

  • Platforms you connect: LinkedIn, Reddit, Telegram, X/Twitter, etc., receive the content and metadata needed to publish posts.
  • AI providers (optional): If you enable AI features, prompts/content may be sent to an AI model provider (e.g., OpenAI) to generate or revise copy.
  • Hosting/automation tools: Your workflows may run on services such as n8n Cloud or your own server. These providers may process data as part of hosting.
  • Legal: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of the Service.

Data Retention

We retain content and logs only for as long as needed to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations. Unless you request a longer archive, operational logs are typically retained for up to 90 days and content drafts for up to 30 days. You may request deletion at any time.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, including encrypted credential storage offered by our automation platform. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Request access, correction, export, or deletion of your data.
  • Revoke API access by removing or rotating credentials.
  • Disable optional AI features at any time.

International Transfers

Depending on your hosting and connected services, data may be processed outside your country. We use reputable providers and transfer data only as necessary to provide the Service.

Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the age required by your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date reflects the latest version. Material changes will be noted on this page.
